---

The general terms and conditions (GT&C) regulate commercial relationships.
They inform your customers of their rights and obligations during the sale of your products and/or services.
This obligation of transparency greatly reduces the risk of dispute between your customers and yourself.
The mandatory nature and content of the general terms and conditions vary depending on whether your offer of goods or services is aimed at individuals or professionals.
